🌱 About EcoPrompt AI

EcoPrompt AI is a smart sustainability-focused AI assistant designed to reduce unnecessary AI computation by optimizing user prompts before generating responses. By making prompts shorter, clearer, and more efficient, it helps save computational resources while maintaining high-quality AI results.

Our mission is simple: Smarter Prompts. Greener Future. Every optimized prompt contributes toward reducing energy consumption and promoting responsible AI usage. Together, we can make technology more efficient and environmentally friendly.
